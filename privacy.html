Privacy Policy

The Short Version

We don't collect anything. Lumina Focus is designed to respect your privacy completely. All your data stays on your device. We don't track you, we don't collect information, and we don't ask for anything.

block No Data Collection

Lumina Focus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This includes:

  • check_circle No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • check_circle No usage analytics or tracking
  • check_circle No location data
  • check_circle No cookies or tracking pixels
  • check_circle No third-party integrations that collect data

smartphone Everything Stays on Your Device

All your timer settings, preferences, and any data you create while using Lumina Focus is stored locally on your device. We have no servers collecting your information. Your productivity data is yours and yours alone.

person_off No Account Required

You don't need to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use Lumina Focus. Just download the app and start focusing. It's that simple.

ad_off No Advertisements

Lumina Focus contains no advertisements. We don't show you ads, and we don't share any data with advertisers because we don't have any data to share.

update Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page. However, our commitment to not collecting your data will remain unchanged.
